Welcome,

This installer will setup and configure Event Publish on your server.

Installation is a simple 2 step process and you will soon be on your way to publishing events on your website.

 

Step 1: Preparing for Installation

In preparation for the installation, please take a moment to do the following:

1. Make the file dbconfig.php in the eventpublish directory writable.

2. Collect your MySQL hostname, username and password information and have them in hand for the next step.

3. Create a new database in MySQL. (If you plan to use an existing database, you can skip this step).
